Name of QuantLet: HMD_ES

Published in: 'Finite-Sample Precision Limits for Expected Shortfall Forecast Comparisons'

Description: 'Implements a precision-audit framework for Expected Shortfall forecast comparison under effective tail sample-size scarcity. The main pipeline computes rolling-window Fissler-Ziegel recalibration across 24 global assets, four forecasters (GJR-GARCH-t, TimesFM 2.5, Chronos-Small, Moirai 2.0), and three tail levels. Diagnostics include a plug-in precision benchmark, a finite-sample sample-size rule, a precision-fragile pairwise comparison screen, and a VaR-first diagnostic for excess ES dispersion.'

Keywords: 'Expected Shortfall, Value-at-Risk, effective tail sample size, precision-fragile comparison, Fissler-Ziegel score, sample-size rule, minimax lower bound, rolling window, recalibration, GARCH, foundation models, tail risk, FRTB, finite-sample inference'
